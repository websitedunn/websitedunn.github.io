New Teams

Hockey, The best sport known to man pt.4

As you grow older, new team members come to be, people move away and best friends are no longer by your side. Over the past sixteen years I have been on a handful of new teams, in new states, playing with new kids. Yet no matter what, our goal was the same, Be the best, while that was on the front of our mind we has no idea we were forming an inseperable bond along the way.
